    • About :

    Welcome to The Bangalore High School, where our purpose is to bring out the best in every student; and help them approach life with confidence, curiosity and consideration for others. The BST community is supportive and friendly and is committed to the pursuit of excellence. We have a long-established reputation for success that is based on collaboration and high standards. The school has also developed an reputation for high-quality holistic education over many years. Established in 1989 in central Tokyo with a small initial cohort, the school now comprises over 65 different nationalities, over 1000 students and 200 staff located on two very well resourced campuses.
